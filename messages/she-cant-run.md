    <h2>She can't run</h2>
    <div id="message-content"></div>
</section>

When the only person left  
to root for her  
is her.

She just can’t  
be on those  
blurry street corners anymore.

She can say,  
“What’s done  
is done.”

She can pull  
the trigger  
of that empty gun.

She can hide  
from the past  
but she can’t run.
